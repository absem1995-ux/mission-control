# Content Formats & What Works

## By Hook Type

### Narratives (40% of mix)

**Pattern:** Problem → Solution → Result

**Example:** "I spent 10 hours/week on repetitive tasks. Built an agent. Now 0."

**Structure:**
- Slide 1: The pain (3-4 seconds)
- Slide 2: The realization (2-3 seconds)
- Slide 3: The solution (3-4 seconds)
- Slide 4: The result (2-3 seconds)
- Slide 5: CTA (1-2 seconds)

**Works best on:** TikTok, Instagram, YouTube Shorts

**Key metrics:** Completion rate (how many watch til end), shares

---

### Tutorials (30% of mix)

**Pattern:** Question → Steps → Takeaway

**Example:** "3 ways to automate your email management in 5 minutes"

**Structure:**
- Slide 1: Hook + promise (2 seconds) "Watch to learn 3 ways..."
- Slide 2: Method 1 (3-4 seconds)
- Slide 3: Method 2 (3-4 seconds)
- Slide 4: Method 3 (3-4 seconds)
- Slide 5: Bonus tip or summary (2 seconds)
- Slide 6: CTA (1-2 seconds)

**Works best on:** All platforms, especially LinkedIn + YouTube

**Key metrics:** Save rate, comment rate (people asking questions)

---

### Showcases (20% of mix)

**Pattern:** Problem → Demo → Benefit

**Example:** "This agent handles 50 customer support tickets per day"

**Structure:**
- Slide 1: Hook (2 seconds) "Watch this agent work..."
- Slides 2-4: Live demo or results (8-10 seconds total)
- Slide 5: Technical detail (optional, 2-3 seconds)
- Slide 6: Benefits + CTA (2 seconds)

**Works best on:** All platforms

**Key metrics:** Click-through rate (CTR), traffic driven to website

---

### Engagement (10% of mix)

**Pattern:** Question → Encourage replies

**Example:** "What's the most annoying repetitive task you do daily?"

**Structure:**
- Slide 1: The question with context (3-4 seconds)
- Slide 2: Visual representation or examples (2-3 seconds)
- Slide 3: CTA to comment/reply (2 seconds)

**Works best on:** TikTok, Instagram

**Key metrics:** Comment count, comment sentiment

## By Platform Content Adaptation

### TikTok Native Format
- 9:16 vertical
- 6 slides max, 3-5 seconds each
- Bold text overlays (white with black outline)
- Trending audio MANDATORY
- Fast pacing, quick cuts
- Music should match hook tone

### Instagram Reels Format
- 9:16 vertical (some 4:5 tolerance)
- 4-5 slides, slightly slower pacing
- Instagram-style text (centered, larger font)
- Trending audio from Instagram library
- More polished production
- Music transitions between cuts

### YouTube Shorts Format
- 9:16 vertical
- Same as Instagram (algorithm is different, format is not)
- YouTube-style CTAs ("Subscribe", "Watch full video")
- End cards with channel branding
- Music from YouTube Audio Library (free, safer)
- Slower pacing than TikTok

### LinkedIn Format
- 1:1 square (can be 4:5)
- Slower, more deliberate pacing
- Professional text overlays
- Optional video (text posts often perform better)
- Longer captions (200+ characters)
- Document posts (PDFs) outperform everything
- No trending audio (LinkedIn users mute)

### Reddit Format
- Video: 4:3 aspect ratio preferred (some 16:9 accepted)
- Text posts: Long-form, 2000+ characters common
- Title: 300 chars max, must be intriguing
- First comment should have context (Redditors hate mystery)
- No music (Reddit mutes videos by default)

### Facebook Format
- Reels: 9:16 (same as Instagram/YouTube)
- Feed posts: 16:9 (wider format)
- Stories: 9:16
- Video: Can be up to 4 hours (most don't exceed 3 min)
- Captions matter (Facebook users read more than TikTok users)
- CTAs explicit ("Join", "Learn More", "Comment Below")

## Slide Design Principles

### Text Overlay Best Practices

**Font:**
- Sans-serif (cleaner on video)
- Bold weight (better readability)
- Minimum 40px font size (mobile view)

**Color:**
- White text with 2-3px black outline (readable on any background)
- High contrast (accessibility + retention)
- No more than 2 colors per slide

**Positioning:**
- Top third or bottom third (avoids covering faces)
- Center-aligned for emphasis
- Left-aligned for narrative feel

**Length:**
- Max 10 words per line
- Max 2-3 lines per slide
- One idea per slide

### Image Generation Prompts

**For Narratives:**
```
"Before and after illustration: [problem] transforming into [solution]. 
Professional design, clean aesthetic, subtle gradient background."
```

**For Tutorials:**
```
"Step-by-step infographic showing [process]. 
Numbered steps (1, 2, 3), icons, clean layout, bright colors."
```

**For Showcases:**
```
"Product screenshot/demo showing [feature]. 
Modern dashboard design, data visualization, professional UI."
```

**For Engagement:**
```
"Thought-provoking illustration of [topic]. 
Abstract art style, engaging colors, conversation-starter vibes."
```

## Video Duration Guidelines

### By Platform

| Platform | Ideal Duration | Max Duration |
|----------|---|---|
| TikTok | 15-30 sec | 10 min |
| Instagram Reels | 21-34 sec | 90 sec |
| YouTube Shorts | 15-60 sec | 60 sec |
| LinkedIn Video | Any | Any (3-5 min common) |
| Reddit Video | 15-90 sec | 15 min |
| Facebook Reels | 15-90 sec | 90 sec |

### Why Duration Matters

- **Short videos (15-30 sec):** Highest completion rate, best for TikTok algorithm
- **Medium videos (30-60 sec):** Good engagement, time for more storytelling
- **Long videos (2-5 min):** Better for LinkedIn, reduces reach on TikTok/Instagram

**Rule:** Don't pad content to fill time. Keep videos as short as possible while telling the full story.

## Call-to-Action (CTA) Examples

### By Platform & Hook Type

**TikTok Narratives:**
- "Follow for more automation hacks"
- "Part 2 if this gets 50K views"
- "Link in bio to join our Discord"

**Instagram Reels:**
- "Drop a 🔥 if you'd use this"
- "Comment which method you'll try first"
- "Tag someone who needs this automation"

**YouTube Shorts:**
- "Subscribe for more AI tutorials"
- "Watch the full version on my channel"
- "Like + Comment which video to make next"

**LinkedIn Posts:**
- "What automation would change your workflow? Comment below"
- "Share this with a colleague who's drowning in manual tasks"
- "Connect if you're building AI agents"

**Reddit:**
- "What's your take? Comment below"
- "This was a game-changer for me. Ever used [tool]?"
- "Curious what the community thinks"

## Performance Metrics by Hook Type

Based on typical social media performance:

### Narratives
- Avg Views: 10K-20K
- Engagement Rate: 5-8%
- Completion Rate: 60-75%
- Shares: Moderate

### Tutorials
- Avg Views: 5K-15K
- Engagement Rate: 8-12% (high comments)
- Completion Rate: 65-80%
- Saves: Very high (people bookmark for later)

### Showcases
- Avg Views: 8K-18K
- Engagement Rate: 6-10%
- Completion Rate: 70-85%
- CTR: 2-5% (traffic to website/product)

### Engagement
- Avg Views: 3K-8K (lower reach, less algorithm favor)
- Engagement Rate: 15-25% (high comments relative to views)
- Completion Rate: 40-60% (people drop after question)
- Comments: Very high (that's the goal)
