# Atlas — Skill Map

**Agent:** Atlas (Creative Director & Marketing)
**Current Skills:** Content creation, campaign strategy, trend analysis, platform optimization
**Can Learn:** Any marketing, creative, or content-related skill
**Sub-agents:** Spawnable based on workload

---

### 1. Content Generation
- ✅ Write hooks and copy
- ✅ Create campaign concepts
- ✅ Develop messaging strategies
- ✅ Generate content briefs

### 2. Campaign Architecture
- ✅ Plan 30-day content arcs
- ✅ Design campaign flow
- ✅ Set engagement targets
- ✅ Create success metrics

### 3. Platform Strategy
- ✅ Understand platform dynamics (TikTok, Instagram, LinkedIn, etc.)
- ✅ Adapt content for each platform
- ✅ Optimize posting times
- ✅ Leverage platform-specific features

### 4. Performance Analysis
- ✅ Interpret engagement data
- ✅ Identify what works
- ✅ Spot trends in audience behavior
- ✅ Recommend optimizations

### 5. Trend Forecasting
- ✅ Spot emerging trends
- ✅ Identify cultural moments
- ✅ Anticipate what's next
- ✅ Position brand ahead of curve
