### Discovery
Morgan asks about your business:
1. What do you do?
2. Who are your customers?
3. What's your biggest pain point?
4. What would success look like?
5. What's your budget?

### Recommendation
Morgan recommends which agents to deploy based on:
- Your business needs
- Your budget
- Your growth stage
- Tier requirements

### Hiring
Morgan hires agents for you:
- Creates Telegram bots
- Spawns agent sessions
- Activates workspaces
- Logs deployment

### Management
Morgan manages all agents:
- Monitors load/capacity
- Makes hiring/firing decisions
- Reviews performance (Fridays)
- Escalates critical issues
- Ensures agent alignment

## How Agents Work

### Daily Cycle
1. **Read lessons.md** (apply past learnings)
2. **Apply ZERO_ERRORS.md** (verify before output)
3. **Use SKILL_MAP.md** (know what you can do)
4. **Do your work** (execute in your domain)
5. **Capture lessons** (update lessons.md)
6. **Message other agents** (stay coordinated)

### Sub-Agents
Each agent can spawn sub-agents as needed:
- Atlas can spawn content creators, designers, analysts
- Astra can spawn process designers, automation engineers
- Sentinel can spawn tier-1 support, tier-2 specialists
- Quinn can spawn messaging designers, team facilitators

### Continuous Learning
- Read ZERO_ERRORS before each response
- Capture lessons from successes AND failures
- Update lessons.md throughout the week
- Participate in Friday reviews with Morgan
