# MARKET_VALIDATION.md — Opportunity Analysis

## Market Size & Demand

### Target Audience
**Solopreneurs + SMBs who need content marketing automation:**
- Coaches (career, fitness, business)
- Consultants (management, marketing, tech)
- Micro-SaaS founders (under $100K ARR)
- Content creators (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram)
- Service providers (design, writing, development)
- E-commerce sellers

**TAM (Total Addressable Market):**
- 4.4M solopreneurs in US
- 33.2M SMBs globally
- Targeting: Solopreneurs + SMBs with $10K+/mo revenue
- **Estimated TAM: 500K-1M potential users**

### Price Sensitivity
Solopreneurs willing to pay:
- **$9-19/mo** for single-platform tools
- **$29-49/mo** for multi-platform automation
- **$99-299/mo** for white-label/custom

Atlas positioning: **$29-49/mo tier** (multi-platform, AI-assisted).

- **Total: $20.7K/mo at scale**

**Timeline:** 18-24 months to reach

## User Acquisition Channels

### Free/Low-Cost
1. **Product Hunt** — Launch Atlas, aim for #1 trending
   - Reach: 100K+ eyes
   - Expected conversion: 50-100 signups
   - Effort: 2 days setup + launch day

2. **Twitter/X Growth** — Content around automation, marketing
   - Post daily about: building tools, marketing tips, automation
   - Build audience 5K+ → 20K+ over 6 months
   - Conversion: 0.1-1% of followers

3. **Indie Hacker Community** — Show Building in Public
   - Post progress weekly
   - Get mentioned in newsletters
   - Reach: 50K+ people monthly

4. **Content Marketing** — Blog about social media automation
   - "How to post 100 times/month without burning out"
   - "TikTok growth hacks for solopreneurs"
   - Aim for Google ranking, SEO traffic

### Paid Channels
1. **Twitter Ads** — Target coaches, creators, entrepreneurs
   - CPM: $5-10, CTR: 0.5-1%
   - Cost per signup: $2-5
   - ROI: +5x at $29/mo price

2. **Google Search Ads** — Target "social media scheduling" keywords
   - CPC: $0.50-2.00, conversion rate: 3-5%
   - Cost per signup: $15-30
   - ROI: Break-even to +2x

3. **Facebook/Instagram Ads** — Retargeting + lookalike audiences
   - CPM: $0.50-2.00, CTR: 0.5-1%
   - Cost per signup: $5-15
   - ROI: +2-3x

### Affiliate Program
- **Commission:** 30% lifetime value or 20% per month
- **Partners:** Content creators, marketing newsletters, influencers
- **Reach:** 100K+ through existing audiences

## Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-----------|
| **Low willingness to pay** | -80% revenue | Validate pricing with surveys first |
| **Postiz API changes** | Breaks feature | Build fallback posting methods |
| **Platform bans** | Can't post | Start with TikTok + Instagram, expand slowly |
| **Market saturation** | Hard to differentiate | Focus on AI generation + analytics (unique) |
| **High churn** | Need constant new users | Build loyalty via automation + results |
| **API costs exceed price** | Negative unit economics | Monitor costs, optimize prompts, set limits |
